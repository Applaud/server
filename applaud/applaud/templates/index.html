connect with your environment

Apatapa connects people with their current location. Using our website, businesses decide what a customer sees when they open Apatapa on their premises. The beta version provides businesses with three customizable features: a newsfeed, employee evaluations, and a feedback center. Newsfeed items are the first thing a customer sees when opening Apatapa — an announcement about a sale, a future event, or a new improvement. Businesses can choose how their individual employees are evaluated and view results in the analytics center. In the feedback center, businesses create questions and use the responses to improve customer experience and build loyalty.
